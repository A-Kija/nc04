Raccon

Raccoons are small mammals native to North America. The raccoon is the largest of the procyonid family, having a body length of 40 to 70 cm and a body weight of 5 to 26 kg. Its grayish coat mostly consists of dense underfur which insulates it against cold weather.

Bunny

Bunny is very sweet and funny animal. It is a very popular pet. It is very soft and furry. It likes to eat carrots and to play with balls of wool. It is very fast and it can jump very high. It is very good at hiding. It is very good at digging holes.
